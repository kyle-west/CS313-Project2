How do I add a new transaction to my books?

At the top of the page there is a circular button with a " + " sign in the center. Click this, fill out the form, and click "Add to MoneyBook". When you are done adding new values. Click "Done" or the arrow button at the top of the page.


How do I edit part of a transaction?

If you wish to change anything in the books, just double click on the part you wish to change. As soon as you are done, it will update and save your progress imediately.


How do I delete a transaction?

For the sake of encouraging integrity in the books, we made it so that you cannot "delete" a row. However, you may disable a row by hovering over it on the right side. A little " ✖ " button will appear. Click this and the row will be counted out of the main balance. To re-enable the row, just do the same thing, this time clicking the " ✔ " button.
